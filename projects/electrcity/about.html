How calculations work

For each device:

  1. Daily Wh = Watts × Hours per day
  2. Monthly Wh = Daily Wh × Days per month
  3. Monthly cost (COP) = Monthly Wh × Cost per Wh

All devices are editable. Presets are common household devices in Colombia.
